AI.Y

AI.Y is a free AI chatbot you can add to your server, you can choose different personas and also generate images with many different models.
AI.Y also supports practically all text/code attachments. Along with text-to-speech, if you are in a voice channel that the bot has access to (It cannot listen).
(Name may differ in Discord but will always be referred to as AI.Y)
